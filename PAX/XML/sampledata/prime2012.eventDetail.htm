Hey Ash, Whatcha Playin’?: Season Four Fast Four Fourious

Intrigue! Suspense! Excitement! The cast and crew of “Hey Ash, Whatcha Playin’?” will probably not elicit any of these emotions as they answer your questions and talk about what they’ve learned throughout the course of producing the show. Come for the premiere of a new HAWP episode, stay for Papa B’s insight and wisdom.

Panelists:

Ashly Burch [actor, co-writer, Hey Ash LLC], Anthony Burch [actor, co-writer, Hey Ash LLC], David Burch [actor, Hey Ash LLC], Ashley Davis [actor, Hey Ash LLC], Justin Yngelmo [co-writer, editor, director, DP, Hey Ash LLC], Grant Cushman [actor, stunt coordinator, Hey Ash LLC], Ulisses Gurgel [actor, stunt coordinator, Hey Ash LLC], Tamara Bruketta [actor, dance choreographer, Hey Ash LLC]
